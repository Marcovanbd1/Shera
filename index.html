Neighbourhood Signals

A neighbourhood-level listening and navigation layer that sits before people become “patients”, at the moment of uncertainty where harm, escalation, and inequity most often begin.

The moment the system cannot hear

Not at appointments. Not at referrals. Not at records. But before any of that. The moment someone notices something is wrong, but doesn’t yet know what to do.

In the wild

Neighbourhood Signals is designed to travel, quietly and safely, into everyday places.

This is what a “live door” looks like in practice, a simple physical access point that captures the moment of uncertainty outside clinical settings.
